Privacy Policy

Last updated: 27 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ProChefDesk ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our application available at prochefdesk.com (the "Service").

ProChefDesk is operated by Ahmet Kaya, an individual based in Perth, Western Australia. This is currently a personal, non-commercial project — there is no company entity behind it.

Plain-language summary: We store your recipes and account info to make the app work. We never sell your data. You can download or delete your data anytime. We use Cloudflare, Supabase, Google Fonts, and jsDelivr to operate the Service.

1. Information we collect

1.1 Account information

When you sign up, we collect:

  • Email address — used for sign-in and account recovery
  • Password — stored as a one-way cryptographic hash via Supabase Auth. We never see or store your plain-text password.
  • Display name (optional) — shown on printed recipes and menus

1.2 Content you create

When you use the Service, you create content that we store on your behalf:

  • Recipes, ingredients, menus, events, suppliers, inventory levels, shopping lists, kitchen card layouts, checklists, and stock count history
  • Workspace configuration (name, location, role, period)
  • Optional chef profile fields (workplace, bio, country)
  • Photos you upload to recipes (stored as data URLs in your account)

1.3 Technical data

We automatically receive:

  • IP address — processed by Cloudflare for security and rate limiting
  • Browser and device info — used only for compatibility and bug diagnosis
  • Usage events — basic in-app actions (e.g. last sync time) stored locally on your device

We do not use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any third-party advertising or behavioural tracking.

2. How we use information

  • To provide the Service (display your recipes, sync to cloud, etc.)
  • To authenticate your account and prevent unauthorized access
  • To respond to support requests sent to hello@prochefdesk.com
  • To diagnose and fix bugs you report through the in-app "Report an issue" form
  • To improve the Service over time

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your recipes or content for marketing without permission
  • Share your data with advertisers
  • Use AI training on your content

3. Third-party services

The Service relies on a small number of trusted third parties to operate. Each handles a specific function:

ServicePurposeData shared
Supabase (supabase.com) Authentication, database, cloud sync Email, hashed password, all your content
Cloudflare (cloudflare.com) Hosting, CDN, security, email routing IP address, request metadata

Each provider has its own privacy policy. You can review them on their respective websites.

4. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored on Supabase servers (hosted on AWS infrastructure). The exact region depends on the Supabase project configuration. Cloud sync transmits your data over encrypted HTTPS connections.

A copy of your data is also stored locally in your browser via localStorage for offline access. You can clear this anytime via your browser settings.

5. Data retention

  • Active accounts: we keep your data as long as your account is active
  • Deleted items: when you delete a recipe, ingredient, or other item, it goes to Trash and is automatically purged after 30 days
  • Account deletion: if you wish to permanently delete your account and all data, contact hello@prochefdesk.com. We will action your request within 30 days
  • Inactive accounts: accounts inactive for 24 months may be flagged for review and you will be contacted before any deletion

6. Your rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access — view all your data inside the app at any time
  • Export — download a complete backup file via Account → "Download backup"
  • Correct — edit any data directly within the app
  • Delete — delete individual items, workspaces, or your entire account
  • Restrict / object — stop using the Service and request data deletion
  • Portability — your backup file is in JSON format, machine-readable

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@prochefdesk.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption on all connections
  • Passwords are hashed (we never see them in plain text)
  • Cloud database is access-controlled via Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Public share links you create only expose the specific recipe or menu you choose, never your full account

However, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. We recommend you keep your password strong and unique.

8. Children

The Service is intended for users 16 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 16. If you believe a child has registered, contact hello@prochefdesk.com and we will delete the account.

9. International users

The Service is operated from Australia. By using the Service, you understand your data may be transferred to and stored in countries other than your own (specifically wherever Supabase and Cloudflare host their infrastructure). We rely on these providers' compliance frameworks (e.g. GDPR for EU users, where applicable).

10. Cookies and local storage

We use:

  • localStorage — to keep your recipes and settings on your device for offline use and faster loading
  • Authentication cookies / tokens — to keep you signed in (managed by Supabase)
  • Cloudflare cookies — for security and performance (essential)

We do not use tracking, advertising, or analytics cookies.

11. Public share links

If you generate a public share link for a recipe or menu, that specific item becomes accessible to anyone with the link. The shared content is a snapshot — only what you chose to share, never your full account or other recipes. You can revoke shares by contacting us.

12. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via the app or the email associated with your account. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.
