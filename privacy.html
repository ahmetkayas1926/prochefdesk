Where the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies, we process your personal data on the following bases:

We use your personal data solely to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Service;
  • Authenticate your account and protect it from unauthorised access;
  • Communicate essential service notices, including security alerts and changes to this Policy;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not use your content to train machine learning models. We do not display advertising in the Service.

Public sharing (Discover). ProChefDesk includes an optional community feature called Discover. View counts and likes are recorded. You can make a recipe private again at any time, which removes it from Discover. Please publish only content you are authorised to share.

Your account data and service content are stored using Supabase Inc., which provides our authentication and database infrastructure.   • Stripe (Stripe, Inc. / Stripe Payments Australia Pty Ltd) — processes subscription payments if you upgrade to a paid plan. Stripe receives your payment details directly; we receive only a customer identifier and your subscription status.

These providers are bound by their own data protection commitments and applicable Standard Contractual Clauses for international transfers.

We retain your personal data for as long as your account remains active. When you delete an item within the Service, it is moved to a recoverable trash for thirty (30) days before permanent deletion.

When you delete your account, we initiate permanent deletion of your account data within thirty (30) days. Encrypted operational backups may retain residual data for up to a further thirty (30) days, after which they are overwritten in the normal course of business.
