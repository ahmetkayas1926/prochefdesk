Why ProChefDesk

Most kitchen software is built for accountants. ProChefDesk is built for the chefs who run the kitchen.

The back-office side of a kitchen usually lives in disconnected spreadsheets — files that never reconcile, recipes stranded on one laptop, formulas that break the moment a supplier price moves. ProChefDesk consolidates it: costing, menus, prep, events, inventory and HACCP in one system that stays in sync from the kitchen to the office.

Try it free, then run your kitchen on Pro. The free plan lets you cost a few dishes and see how the system works. Pro removes the limits — unlimited recipes, cloud sync across devices, HACCP, labour cost and clean exports — for one flat price you can cancel anytime.
