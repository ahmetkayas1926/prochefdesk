The Idea

Most kitchen software is built for accountants — endless menus, exports, login screens.

ProChefDesk is built for the line. Open a recipe with one tap. Update a price and your food cost recalculates instantly. Print a kitchen card during service. Plan a wedding for 200 guests in twenty minutes.

No subscriptions. No upsells. Just tools that respect your time.

The Toolset

Ten tools.
One kitchen brain.

01

Recipes

Live food cost, allergen tracking, photo, version history. Built around how a chef actually thinks.

02

Ingredients

Suppliers, prices, units, yield percent. Update one price; every recipe cost updates instantly.

Sections, prices, allergens, profit margins. Print or share a public link in two taps.

A4-printable recipe cards for the line. Big text, scaled portions, allergens highlighted.

Scale any recipe for any guest count. Allergen-safe quantities, rounded to your unit of choice.

06

Shopping List

Pulled from your menus or events. Grouped by supplier, ready for WhatsApp or email.

Stock counts with variance tracking. Spot waste before it eats your margin.

08

Suppliers

Contacts, products, lead times. One-tap call, email, or WhatsApp. Order history at a glance.

09

Events & Catering

Checklists

Opening, closing, HACCP, cooling logs. Templates included; customise per station.
